Transform Tier-1 operations by automating repetitive requests, enabling intelligent self-service, and freeing engineers to focus on complex, high-value work that requires human expertise.

Quarterly Milestones

  • Q2 (Apr-Jun 2026): Deploy first 2 automation solutions (password reset + access requests). Target: 10% automated resolution rate.
